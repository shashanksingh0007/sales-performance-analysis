Superstore Sales Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data to evaluate business performance across regions, product categories, and time periods. The objective is to identify sales trends, top and bottom-performing products, regional performance differences, and seasonal patterns that can support data-driven business decisions.

Dataset

Source: Kaggle – Superstore Sales Dataset
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhanupratapbiswas/superstore-sales

Project Objectives
Analyze sales performance across regions, categories, and time.
Identify best-performing and worst-performing products.
Discover seasonal sales trends and patterns.
Evaluate profitability and business performance using key metrics.
Generate actionable business recommendations.

Key Insights
Certain categories contribute significantly more revenue than others.
A small group of products generates a large portion of total sales.
Regional performance varies considerably across the business.
Seasonal peaks indicate opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.
Some products generate low or negative profit despite strong sales volume.
Business Recommendations
Focus marketing efforts on high-profit product categories.
Reduce discounts on consistently loss-making products.
Improve inventory planning during peak sales seasons.
Develop targeted campaigns for underperforming regions.
Bundle low-performing products with best-selling products to increase sales.
